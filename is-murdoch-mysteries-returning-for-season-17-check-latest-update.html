Where To Watch Murchod Mysteries?

The seventeenth season of Murdoch Mysteries is scheduled to premiere in the fall of 2023 in Canada on Citytv, CBC, and CBC Gem.

There will be a total of 24 episodes in the season, and each one will run for one hour. The season will also be broadcast on Alibi in the United Kingdom and Acorn TV in the United States. Both of these networks will carry it.

Fans can catch up on previous seasons or read the books by Maureen Jennings that served as the show’s inspiration as they wait for an official announcement.
