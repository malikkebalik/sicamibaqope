Pregnant Ashley Benson marries oil heir Brandon Davis: report

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 2, 2024

Ashley Benson has reportedly said “I do.”

According to “Entertainment Tonight,” the actress, 33, has married her fiancé, Brandon Davis, with whom she is expecting her first child.

Sources told the outlet on Wednesday that the couple is now legally wed.

Confirmation of the purported nuptials comes just a few days after Ashley’s mother, Shannon Benson, posted a photo of what appeared to be the pair’s wedding bands.

In an Instagram carousel that was partially captioned, “#octoberwasgood,” Shannon included a closeup shot of what looked like Ashley’s left hand with a gold band alongside her massive, oval-shaped engagement ring.

Another left hand, presumably Davis’, showed a matching gold band — also on that finger.

Reps for Ashley did not immediately respond to Page Six’s request for comment.

The “Pretty Little Liars” star and her oil heir beau announced their engagement in July.

“Love of my life @ashleybenson,” Brandon, the grandson of billionaire oil tycoon Marvin Davis, captioned an Instagram Story that showed off the enormous sparkler he had just given his leading lady.

.

Ashley reposted the snap and added, “My best frienddddd I love you.”

The darling duo’s relationship first made headlines in February, though they had been spotted together numerous times before that.

As we recently confirmed, Ashley is also pregnant.

“She’s around the four-month [mark] and so excited to be a mom,” a source told Us Weekly last month. “She’s been eating really well and taking care of her body.”

The insider made sure to note that the “Spring Breakers” star’s family “is also thrilled for her.”
