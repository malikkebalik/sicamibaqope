Kimbo Slice was a Bahamian mixed martial artist, boxer, professional wrestler, and occasional actor who gained popularity for the mutual combat street fights which were spread across the Internet. In 2007, he signed a professional contract with EliteXC after leaving the underground fighting scene. Kimbo Slice competed with Houston Alexander in his UFC debut in The Ultimate Fighter: Heavyweights finale and emerged as the winner. Slice had also appeared in a handful of movies including The Scorpion King 3: Battle for Redemption, Blood and Bone, Merry Christmas, Drake & Josh, Dawg Fight, and Circle of Pain.

Education

Kimbo Slice studied at Bel-Air Elementary School before attending Cutler Ridge Middle School and Richmond Heights Middle School. He then got enrolled at the Miami Palmetto High School, where he was the star middle linebacker.

Afterward, Kimbo Slice joined both Bethune-Cookman University and the University of Miami, where he held an athletic scholarship and studied criminal justice.

    Kimbo Slice Facts
