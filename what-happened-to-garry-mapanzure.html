Who is Garry Mapanzure?

The late Garry Mapanzure was one of the most renowned figures in Zimbabwe’s music industry mainly because of his appealing Afropop tunes.

He kick-started his music journey in 2017 and soon released several breathtaking singles that gave him a distinct place in the Afro-pop genre.

In describing this fusion, he used the term “Afropop.” This made him versatile in integrating all kinds of music into his work.

Born in Zimbabwe under the birth name Garry Mapanzure, he is celebrated for giving his unique flavor to live music by creating an electrifying and contagious beat.

His revolutionary way of composing and producing music gained a substantial following, bringing him to the limelight in the Zimbabwe music arena.

He embraced the rich culture behind Afropop and created his niche by mixing traditional elements with contemporary rhythms in a musician mix, which attracted both within Zimbabwe and outside fans.
