BIOGRAPHY OF CEASER EMANUEL

Ceaser Emanuel is an American tattoo artist, owner, and reality TV star. He is known for being the owner of a tattoo shop which is featured on the VH1 reality show ‘Black Ink Crew’.

Ceaser is the main owner of Black Ink and its other partners are Dutchess Lattimore, Alex Estevez, Puma Robinson, Sky Days, Donna Lombardi, Young Bae and many more.

CEASER EMANUEL EDUCATION

Ceaser graduated from the High School of Graphic Communication and Arts and then majored in Graphic Arts from Katharine Gibbs College in 1999.

CEASER EMANUEL TATOO CAREER

Emanuel of ‘Black Ink Crew’ has always maintained that he got into the tattoo business to provide a better future for his daughter. Life in New York can be difficult, especially for people from lower socioeconomic classes.

As such, you need to be creative and take every opportunity you can to improve your life. He did this by opening a tattoo parlor in Harlem. He gradually becomes a sensation, catching the attention of VH1 producers.

January 2013 marked Emanuel’s reality TV debut when Black Ink Crew premiered.

It featured the artist and his team, as well as all the black celebrities they catered to. The show is in its seventh season as of now and doesn’t seem to be slowing down any time soon. The show would also spawn a spin-off series, Black Ink Crew: Chicago.

CEASER EMANUEL BLACK INK CREW

Black Ink Crew is an American reality television series that aired on VH1 and launched on January 7, 2013. It chronicles the day-to-day operations and staff drama at an African-American owned and operated tattoo shop in Harlem, New York.

The spin-off Black Ink Crew: Chicago premiered on October 26, 2015. On August 20, 2018, the series was renewed for a seventh season, which premiered on September 19, 2018.

On June 27, 2019, it was announced that a new spin-off, titled Black Ink Crew: Compton, will premiere on August 14, 2019.

CEASER EMANUEL AGE

Emanuel was born on June 5, 1979 in New York.

CEASER EMANUEL GIRLFRIEND

The artist’s love life is something he has kept under wraps for the past few years. However, he was once involved with a woman named Crystal, who is the mother of his daughter.

Ceaser also reportedly had a relationship with co-star Cristina Lattimore in 2015. Their relationship ended after allegations of infidelity on Ceaser’s part came to light.

He had apparently impregnated another woman. Ceaser of “Black Ink Crew” would then go on to date with reality TV star, Karlie Redd, who stars on Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta.
