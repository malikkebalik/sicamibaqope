She Admitted to Using Ozempic!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 12, 2024

Marlo Hampton has undergone a noticeable weight loss in the past few years. In 2018, the Real Housewives of Atlanta star's trainer showed off her physical transformation in her before and after pictures. In 2020, she revealed that she regained the weight she lost and went on a diet. Recently, Marlo Hampton admitted to using Ozempic as an aid to weight loss.

Marlo Hampton is known to be a sassy and self-assured fashion maverick from Bravo's reality series The Real Housewives of Atlanta (She has a high-end fashion boutique in Atlanta, Georgia). The reality star is also the girl we love or love to hate on I Dream of NeNe. She is also a vlogger who operates her own website which she uses to sell her unique and stylish merchandise and clothing line and to write blogs about the latest fashion trends and such alongside all her social media profiles.

She has really cashed on the fame that she found via reality television to grow as a social media influencer and a designer. Also, her outspoken nature and well-timed controversy have contributed to keeping her relevant on social media and fresh in people's minds. She has garnered a huge number of followers that way who take an interest in everything she does and everything that happens to her. Currently, they are interested to know about her weight loss. She seems noticeably slimmer than she was about five years ago and fans are curious to know what happened in all these years. Here's everything to know about Marlo Hampton's weight loss!

Marlo Hampton's Weight Loss: Her Trainer Revealed Her Transformation in Before and After Pictures!

Marlo Hampton (@marlohampton), if you compare her pictures from about five years ago to her recent photos, you will notice that she has undergone a noticeable weight loss. Her figure looks much toned now and she looks a lot shapelier than she has ever looked. Fans are curious to know how she lost weight and enhanced and toned her figure.

Well, as it turns out, the fitness journey of The Real Housewives of Atlanta star has gone through several stages of ups and downs. The first time her fans witnessed her physical transformation was in 2018 when her fitness trainer shared her before and after weight loss pictures on his Instagram page. To say the change in her body was drastic would be sort of an overstatement because she was never overweight in the first place and she didn't look like she had much weight to lose.

But whatever little change it was, it was very significant and noticeable because Marlo Hampton looked much more toned and had a defined body shape in the after picture. That photo of hers made the fans' jaws drop. They were extremely impressed with her weight loss transformation which the trainer revealed took a lot of hard effort and dedication. Marlo Hampton Admitted to Using Ozempic for Weight Loss!

Marlo Hampton admitted to using Ozempic for weight loss.
Image Source: Essence

Marlo Hampton unfortunately was not able to maintain that weight loss for long. She regained all the pounds back during COVID-19. In 2020, she revealed that she had had weight gain after she stepped on the scale while she was on self-quarantine and realized the numbers were not skewed in favor of fitness. She photographed the number - 199.8 - and wrote a caption cussing out the coronavirus and self-quarantine, expressing her frustration, "S--t just got real!!!!" She also added, "Diet starts NOW!"

Marlo Hampton was able to maintain her weight loss right up till the quarantine when she was following her diet routine which included two shakes a day, one protein bar cut in half, two meals that consisted of protein and green vegetables, 96 ounces of water, and three cocktails, consistently. She couldn't continue it while on quarantine and so, she gained all the weight back again.

Marlo Hampton said that she was going to start her dieting again and she might have. She has lost weight since 2020 again but admittedly, it might be due to her use of Ozempic. She opened up about using the generic brand of weight loss drug to get in shape for her new look during the RHOA reunion.
