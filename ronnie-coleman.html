Who exactly is Ronnie Coleman?

Ronnie Coleman is a retired American bodybuilder. He is best known for his eight Mr. Olympia titles.

He is also an actor, having appeared in episodes of 5th Ward (2020) and Criminal Minds (2016).

Age, Parents, Ethnicity, and Education of Ronnie Coleman

Ronald Dean Coleman was born on May 13, 1944, in Monroe, Louisiana, in the United States. His mother’s and father’s names are not printed.

Ronnie comes from an All-American family and has a sister named Jacquelyn Jones.

According to his academic credentials, he attended Grambling State University and earned a BSc in accounting in 1984.

Ronnie Coleman’s Professional Career and Life

Ronnie Coleman has crowned Mr. Texas (Heavyweight & Overall) for the first time in 1990. He went on to win a number of championships and titles as a result of this.

In 1991, he won the World Amateur Championships, the Canada Pro Cup in 1995-96, and the Grand Prix Russia in 1997. Furthermore, he is known for becoming Mr. Olympia not once, but eight times between 1998 and 2005.

Ronnie also appeared on The Joe Rogan Experience (2020), The Untitled Action Bronson Show (2017), The Tonight Show with Jay Leno (2000), and other shows.

He has also appeared in documentaries such as Chris Cormier: I Am the Real Deal (2019), Ronnie Coleman: The King (2018), Dare2Dream: The Flex Wheeler Story (2015), and others.
