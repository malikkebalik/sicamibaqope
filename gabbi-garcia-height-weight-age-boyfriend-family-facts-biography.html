Gabbi Garcia is a Filipino actress, model, and singer, known for Seasons of Love mini TV series that aired in 2014. She also appeared in InstaDad and Let the Love Begin TV series in 2015, as the characters Marikit ‘Kit’ Monteamor and Pia V. Sta. Maria, respectively. Gabbie had a small role in Magpakailanman between 2014 and 2015, and then in Wagas (2015-2016) TV series with Kiko Estrada, Benjamin Alves, Dex Quindoza, and others. She captivated the attention of the audience in 2016 and 2017 with her role of Alena in GMA Network’s reboot of Encantadia.

Gabbie also appeared in Laut in 2016 and had her 1st single released as a singer during that time, named Love You The Way I Do, in collaboration with Ruru Madrid from his album One Heart, signed by GMA Records. In November 2017, she announced and then released All I Need, her 2nd single, that was self-produced this time. In 2018, Gabbie played Lena in Victor Magtanggol TV series, as well as Jade Roces in Pamilya Roces, a comedy-drama family television series. As of February 2019, she has 3.2 million followers on Instagram and 4.35 million followers on Facebook.

    Gabbi Garcia Facts

  • She owned 2 cats, named Elon and Mandy, and had a dog named Sunshine.
  • Gabbi is a big supporter of the LGBTQ community and a body positivity promoter through her social media. She said that her flaws are what makes her unique and that her fans shouldn’t worry over their own body flaws, and learn to embrace them instead.
  • Gabbie plays guitar and has been musically inclined from an early age.
  • Gabbie became a licensed scuba diver when she was only 10 years old.
  • She participated in a variety of beauty pageants. In 2011, she won Ms. Skin White pageant, and 2 years later won Ms. Solane.
  • Before becoming a well-known actress, she used to perform local gigs with various bands in the vicinity in order to fund her acting dreams.
  • Let the Love Begin was one of her most notable appearances and made her well known in 2015, thus giving her a chance to land new gigs. She played the character of Pia V. Sta. Maria.
  • She said that she felt responsible for the image she presents to more than 3 million followers on her social media, with a big part of them being teenagers and kids. Gabbie also said that social media is a powerful outlet to reach a massive audience when used properly and that negative comments shouldn’t pull you down, only motivate you to be authentic.
  • Encantadia TV series by GMA Network was what made her one of the highest sought-after Filipino TV actresses in 2016. It was a reboot of the 2005 series with the same name.
  • Gabbie appeared on both Lip Sync Battle Philippines and Celebrity Bluff TV shows in a span of 2 years, between 2016 and 2018.
  • Gabbi won the “German Moreno Youth Achievement Award” at the 64th FAMAS Awards in 2016. The same year, she took home The PEP List Award for being “Female Stylish Star of the Year”.
  • She met the American model Gigi Hadid in January 2018 during a Maybelline MasterClass event in Tokyo, Japan.
  • Her 2nd film appearance as Alex Balatbat in Meant to Beh (2017) skyrocketed her acting career due to the positive critics and audience reviews.
  • Gabbi missed a press conference for Sherlock Jr. in January 2018. She had to explain to the fans on Twitter that she was in the hospital with a severe kidney infection.
  • She left both Sherlock Jr. TV series and the variety program Sunday PinaSay in early 2018. Even though it was speculated that she was leaving the GMA Network, Gabbie later clarified that she didn’t have enough time to pursue her education on top of various acting projects.
