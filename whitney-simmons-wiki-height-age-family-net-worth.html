Whitney Simmons is an American fitness trainer, model, Youtuber, and social media influencer. She is best known for sharing fitness, health, diet tips, and tutorials on a self-titled Youtube channel. She is also known for sharing her sizzling, attractive, and engaging modeling photos on Instagram.

Whitney grew up in Fresno, California, USA along with her parents and siblings. She went to a local high school for graduation and moved to a university to complete a bachelor’s degree. While studying in high school, she found out her passion for modeling and fitness. She drop out of college in 2nd year and decided to pursue a career as a fitness trainer and model.

Whitney Simmons Age, Height, and Weight

Whitney Simmons’s age is 28 years (as of 2021). She is 5′ 6″ tall and her weight is approximately 58 kg.

Career and Achievements

Whitney started her career as a fitness trainer and model on a self-titled Youtube channel in 2015 after launching the channel. She uploaded a debut video titled 5 tips to achieve your 2016 FITNESS GOALS! in 2016, which has more than 156k views as of now.

Slowly, she accumulated fame on Youtube by sharing fashion, fitness, workout, diet, challenges, lifestyle videos, and travel vlogs. As of now, Whitney has amassed more than 2.06m subscribers on the channel. The most popular video on her channel is “Grow And Define Your BOOTY And LEGS | FULL Workout” with more than 3.5m views.

With increasing popularity as a fitness Youtuber, Whitney garnered millions of followers on different social media platforms including Instagram and Facebook. On Instagram, she has gathered more than 3.2m followers. Subsequently, multiple fashions and sports brands including Gymshark, Alani Nu, and many others have chosen her as a social media promoter of their products.

Besides this, she has been an official athlete of Gymshark Women. Whitney has launched a swimwear brand titled Toluca Swim. Moreover, different fitness magazines and newspaper articles have featured her on their covers.
