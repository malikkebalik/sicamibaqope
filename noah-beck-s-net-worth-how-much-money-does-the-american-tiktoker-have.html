A Look At Noah Beck's Early Life

Social media personality Noah Beck was born on May 4, 2001, in Peoria, Arizona.

While at Peoria, Arizona, he attended Ironwood High School. Beck was athletic from a young age and played for the S.C. del Sol club soccer team in Phoenix.

He gained prominence in the sport early as a team captain in the U.S. Youth Soccer Olympic Development Program in Arizona from 2014 to 2017. Beck then moved to Utah during his last two high school years, where the social media personality played for the Real Salt Lake Academy.

In 2019, the social media personality attended the University of Portland, where he received a full-ride scholarship for his soccer skills. Beck also played as a midfielder in the Portland Pilots men's soccer. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic in Portland, Oregon, Beck completed the second semester of his first year online before dropping out due to online success.
