Wes Morgan is an English former professional footballer who is best known for his career as a defender for teams like Nottingham Forest, the Jamaican national team, Leicester City, as well as the Kidderminster Harriers. During the course of his career, he has won numerous tournaments such as Premier League, FA Cup, Football League Championship, and has received honors like Leicester City‘s “Player of the Season”.

    Westley Morgan Facts

  • During his stay at Nottingham Forest (2002-2010), Morgan appeared in a total of 402 matches.
  • Morgan became a captain of Leicester City before the start of the 2012-2013 season.
  • On May 2, 2016, he won the 2015-2016 Premier League trophy with Leicester City and so became the first Jamaican to win a Premier League.
  • At the age of 15, he was rejected by Notts County and was about to leave football.
  • On January 20, 2012, Nottingham Forest turned down an offer from Leicester City. However only 10 days after, the Jamaican international signed a contract with Leicester City for a total fee of around 1 million British pounds.
  • In the 2012-2013 season, he was Player of the Month for September.
  • On September 1, 2013, for the first time, Wes was selected for the senior national team of Jamaica.
  • His debut as a captain of Jamaica happened on September 9, 2014, in a 3-1 loss to Canada.
  • Morgan is one of the owners of a tattoo studio called Blue Ink located in Leicester.
  • He announced his retirement in May 2021.
