T-Pain Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 2, 2024

Who is T-Pain married to?

T-Pain is married to Amber Najm. The couple is married since 2003.

Also, the couple has one daughter, Lyria, and two sons, Muziq and Kaydnz Kodah.

Till now, the duo is still happy and inseparable.

Who is T-Pain?

T-Pain is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter.

T-Pain is famous for the soundtrack of Tropic Thunder (2008), Pitch Perfect (2012), and The Lego Movie 2: The Second Part (2019).

As of 2022, he was seen in Cartoon Network’s Cartoonito (TV Series).

T-Pain: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

T-Pain was born on 30th September 1984 in Tallahassee, Florida, USA.

His real name is Faheem Rashad Najm. He is the son of Aliyah Diane Najm and Shasheem Mutee Najm.

Besides that, his father is in the Homeboyz to Men program, meanwhile, his mother is a Bahamian Chef.

Moreover, T-Pain holds American nationality with African- American ethnicity. Moving to T-Pain’s educational background there are no details.

T-Pain: Early Career and Professional life

Early Career

At the age of 3, T-Pain got his first taste of the music business when a friend of the family, Gospel Jazz Ben Tankard, an Artist, and Producer allowed him to spend time and twist the knobs at his recording studio.

Moreover, at the age of 10, he turned his bedroom into a music studio, using a keyboard, a beat machine, and a four-track recorder.

Professional Life

T-Pain joined the rap group Nappy Headz in 2004.

Later, he recorded I’m Fucked Up a cover version of Akon’s single Locked Up.

T-Pain debuted his first studio album, Rappa Trent Sanga released on 6th December 2005.

In 2007, he released his second studio album, Epiphany.

In the following year, he launched his third studio album Three Rinz on 11th November, labeled by Nappy Boy Entertainment.

Moreover, on 6th December 2011 released his fourth studio album Revolver.

In 2017, on 17th November he break out his fifth studio album, Oblivion through Knovict Muzik, RCA Records.

Furthermore, T-Pain released his sixth studio album on 27th February 2019 1UP.

T-Pain: Awards and Nominations

  • T-Pain is the winner of the Grammy Awards for Blame It in 2010.
  • He won the CIC Award for The Lego Movie 2: The Second Part in 2020.
  • T-Pain is the nominee for Grammy Award for Got Money in 2010.
  • He has been nominated for Bet Award for Blame It in 2009.
