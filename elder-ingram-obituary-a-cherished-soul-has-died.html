Elder Ingram Obituary, A Cherished Soul Has Died

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 13, 2024
0SHARESShareTweet

Elder Ingram Obituary, Death – It is with deep sadness that we inform you of the tragic passing of Elder Joe Ingram, the esteemed Coordinator of the International COGIC Gentlemen’s Workshop. Elder Ingram was involved in a car accident shortly after returning from the 115th Holy Convocation.

At this time, details regarding the incident are limited, and we are awaiting further information. As soon as more details emerge, we will ensure to keep you informed. Our hearts go out to Elder Ingram’s family and loved ones during this incredibly challenging time.

Elder Ingram’s contributions to the International COGIC Gentlemen’s Workshop have been invaluable, and his loss is profoundly felt by our community. His dedication and leadership have left an indelible mark, and his absence creates a void that will be difficult to fill.

As we come to terms with this tragedy, let us rally together to support one another and, most importantly, to uplift Elder Ingram’s family in our thoughts and prayers. The road ahead will undoubtedly be tough for them, and our collective strength and compassion can provide a source of solace.

We extend our heartfelt condolences to all who knew and worked alongside Elder Joe Ingram. In this time of mourning, let us remember his legacy and the positive impact he had on our community. Details regarding any memorial services or arrangements will be communicated as they become available.
