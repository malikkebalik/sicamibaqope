Bob Whitfield Wiki Biography

Football is the main source of Bob Whitfield’s estimated net worth, and this is the answer to the popular question about how rich is Bob Whitfield? Nowadays Bob doesn’t play football anymore, however, he is still one of the most known personalities in sport.

Bob Lectress Whitfield III was born on October 18, 1971, in Carson, California, United States. He was interested in football from an early age – he played football at Banning High School together with another subsequently famous American football player Mark Tucker. Later he also was a player while studying in Stanford University, and furthermore, his coach was Dennis Green.

Bob Whitfield started his professional career as a footballer and earning serious net worth in 1992, when he joined the National Football League (NFL). During his career  Bob played about 220 games and earned a huge amount of his net worth thanks to his awesome ability. At first he played for Atlanta Falcons until 2003, however, from 2004 Whitfield  played for the Jacksonville Jaguars – this period of his life lasted three years and added a big amount to his net worth. Unsurprisingly, Whitfield was left sitting on the bench while his team was playing versus the Philadelphia Eagles and Washington Redskins.

This was the end of Bob Whitfield’s chance to increase his net worth while playing football, in 2006, playing for the New York Giants.
