In 2008, Robert, who is famous for his role as Iron Man, was marked as the 100 most influential people in the world by Time magazine. Today let's talk about Exton's family, siblings and other information. 

When Exton Elias Downey was born?

A baby boy, Exton Elias Downey was born on February 7, 2012, in Los Angeles, CA. He was born to Robert and Susan at 7:24 a.m. At the time of his birth, he weighed 7 lbs., and 5oz.

"Everyone's happy and healthy," Downey's spokesperson stated during that time. Recently in Feb. 2021, he celebrated his 9th birthday. Moreover, he has a younger sister Avri Roel Downey, 6, and a half older brother, Indio Falconer Downey, 27.

On the big day, the bride Susan donned the V-shaped simple silk gown that featured a long veil. Similarly, the groom, the groom wore a tuxedo that features a purple scarf. Since the day, Mr and Mrs Downey are sharing a good bond as a perfect married pair.

Previously, Iron Man, Downey Jr. was linked with actress Sarah Jessica Parker, 55, after meeting her in Firstborn' set. Later they parted ways due to his drug addiction.
