Derek From Love After Lockup Sisters Elizabeth and Aubrey

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 16, 2024

Derek from Love After Lockup sisters Elizabeth Cartel and Aubryanna have all appeared on the reality show. They have a conflict with his girlfriend Monique.

29-year-old Warner served nine years of imprisonment for drug possession and distribution in Chicago and Cleveland. Following this, he stayed away from his family for many years.

He was allegedly among the 32 men detained in a drug ring bust in 2013 near Cleveland's Collingwood and Euclid.

After serving nine years of sentence, he returned to the real world and focused all his time and attention on his partner, Monique. However, his sisters were not in favor of their relationship.

After Warner came home from his release, his sisters were constantly arguing with his girlfriend. In Episode 33, Robinson also clashes with his sisters at his release. 

Sister Elizabeth Cartel

Derek Warner Sister Elizabeth Cartel is perhaps best known for being the sister of Love After Lockup star Derek.

She gained public notice following her clash with Monique and her siblings.

Coming from Beachwood, Ohio, Carter currently lives in Atlanta, Georgia, according to her Facebook bio.

The Internet is how she makes money and takes care of the family. She said, growing up, her brother only showed love to his dad's side, and she took care of everything and held it down.

Cartel has always been herself. She is an active social media user and is available on platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, TikTok, and Snapchat.

She is found on Instagram under the username _elizabethcartel. With only 48 posts, she enjoys a fanbase of over 42.3k followers. On Twitter, she goes by the nametag @AzzNaked "The Prettiest Mean Girl."

As a TikToker, she has gained over 5.9k followers and 3.1k likes. She often shares alluring clips on the platform and lip-syncs content. She has also mentioned "Derek Sister" in the bio.
