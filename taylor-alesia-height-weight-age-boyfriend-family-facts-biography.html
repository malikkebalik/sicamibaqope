Boyfriend / Spouse

Taylor Alesia has dated –

  • Taylor Caniff (2015) – Although the rumors of Alesia dating fellow social media personality Taylor Caniff had been doing rounds for quite some time, they had publicly maintained that they were good friends until February 2015, when she posted a picture of them kissing on the beach. Alesia also revealed that they had dated for about 8 months and Caniff and his manager had tried to put pressure on her to get her to retract her claims. She was even threatened with a lawsuit.

However, their relationship didn’t last more than a couple of months. Also, it seems that their breakup was quite messy as they have often taken snide shots at each other on a couple of occasions.
  • Tanner Fox – In April 2017, Tanner Fox posted a picture of him and Taylor on his Instagram to officially reveal that they were dating. Taylor later revealed that she had met YouTuber Tanner Fox just 4 days after having moved back to Arizona. In December 2017, it was alleged that they had gone separate ways. However, they squashed such rumors. In January 2018, they sent their fans into meltdown by revealing that she was pregnant. It was later revealed that it was just a prank. They later broke up.
  • Matt Freeman – She has also been romantically involved with Matt Freeman.
