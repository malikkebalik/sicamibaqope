Early Life of Ace Frehley

Ace Frehley, born on April 27, 1951, in The Bronx, New York City, New York, United States, to Carl Daniel Frehley and Ester Anna Hecht, was introduced to music at an early age. He began playing the electric guitar at the age of 13 and drew inspiration from the likes of Jimi Hendrix, Albert Lee, Buddy Guy, and The Rolling Stones. Frehley has a background in classical guitar and completed his education from DeWitt Clinton High School. Ace is exemplary of the phrase “born to rock.”

Ace Frehley stands tall at 5 feet 9 inches and has a weight of approximately 75 kg. He possesses endearing brown eyes and brown hair. Specific measurements such as chest-waist-hip, dress size, shoe size, and bicep dimensions are not publicly available.

Ace Frehley’s Financial Status

As of February 2024, Ace Frehley’s net worth is recorded at $1 million USD. His earnings as a hard-rock performer range from $80,000 to $100,000 for each event.

Ace Frehley’s Career

Space Ace has left an indelible mark with his frenetic electric-guitar style and has made significant contributions to hard rock and heavy metal music. His solo hits like Fire and Water, Bronx Boy, and Rockin’ With the Boys have garnered acclaim. His recent release, Mission to Mars, was unveiled on May 28th, 2019, accompanied by an animated YouTube video.

Ace Frehley’s Relationships

Ace Frehley was previously married to Jeanette Trerotola in 1976 and has a child with her. He has been in relationships with Wendy Moore, Diane Cusano, Kim Rowe, Audrey Hamilton, and Linda Suseback.
