Overall Meaning

Tyler Childers's song Shake the Frost is a love song that compares the subject of the song to a Sunday in Kentucky. The singer is in love with his partner, and they remind him of home and the warmth of church choirs singing among the trees. He loves her like the mountains, in the way that they welcome the morning. He is grateful for her presence and wishes to express it more often. He confesses that he acts angry most of the time and hides his emotions. However, he promises that if she stays, he would let go of his bottled-up anger and express his love every day. He asks her to stay and shake the frost off his bones.


The song's lyrics poetically symbolize the struggles of love and the importance of communication. The singer recognizes his love's impact on him, but he also acknowledges his flaws and the need to change them. He mentions Mustang as a symbol of his high hopes, but the price of the dreams became too high that he ended up giving up. It could symbolize his fear of expressing his feelings due to past experiences. However, he is willing to let go of that fear for the sake of his love's happiness. The song's overall message can be summarized that love requires sacrifices and vulnerability to thrive.
