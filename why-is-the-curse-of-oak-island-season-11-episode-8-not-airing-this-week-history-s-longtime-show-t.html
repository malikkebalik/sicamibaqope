Why is 'The Curse of Oak Island' Season 11 Episode 8 not airing this week? History's longtime show t

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 4, 2024

NOVA SCOTIA, CANADA: This week, fans of 'The Curse of Oak Island' will have to wait as Season 11 Episode 8 won't be airing on Tuesday, December 26 on the official History Channel. Instead, the time slot is dedicated to 'The Curse of Oak Island: Drilling Down,' a companion series to the long-running show.

In the upcoming episode of 'The Curse of Oak Island: Drilling Down,' titled 'The Lot 5 Enigma' all attention is on Lot 5. The fellowship has been investigating Lot 5 since its purchase by the Lagina brothers over a year ago.

The episode follows Matty Black as he closely examines the clues provided by the lot, striving to unravel their significance. The focal point of the monumental 11th season of the History show is to finally solve the 228-year-old mystery of Oak Island.

When will 'The Curse of Oak Island' Season 11 Episode 8 air?

One can watch 'The Curse of Oak Island' Season 11 Episode 8 on the official History Channel. 

In addition to this, dedicated fans of the show can catch its new episodes on streaming platforms such as Fubo TV, Philo, and DirecTV Stream.

 What to expect from 'The Curse of Oak Island' Season 11 Episode 8?

The title of 'The Curse of Oak Island' Season 11 Episode 8 is 'A Void at All Costs'. In the next episode of History's longtime-running show, Rick and Marty Lagina will be seen experiencing great excitement and happiness when a challenging obstacle leads them to a significant new discovery.

At the time of writing, the channel has not revealed any additional information about the upcoming episode's discovery.

What happened in 'The Curse of Oak Island' Season 11 Episode 7?

'The Curse of Oak Island' Season 11 Episode 7 was named 'The Great Flood.' In the last episode of the series, the Lagina brothers paid a visit to the Garden Shaft for the first time this year, and their hard work paid off.

When the dynamic duo arrived at the underground Garden Shaft, they and their team discovered an ancient booby trap.

'The Curse of Oak Island' Season 11 Episode 8 is set to drop on January 2, 2024, at 9 pm ET on the History Channel.
