Savannah Chrisley's Ex-fianc Nic Kerdiles Still Calls Her a Cute Nickname 3 Months after Split

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Savannah Chrisley updated her fans that she just passed her real estate exam and received many congratulatory messages, including one from her ex-boyfriend Nick Kerdiles, who used an endearing term to call her.

It has been three months since "Chrisley Knows Best" star Savannah Chrisley and Nick Kerdiles ended their relationship, but it seems there is no bad blood between them.

Fans observed earlier this week when Savannah posted a picture on her Instagram page showing off her beautiful face while clad in a gray turtleneck and black jacket.

Savannah Chrisley and Nic Kerdiles celebrate their Engagement on March 27, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images.

In the caption to the post, Savannah revealed to her fans that she just passed her real estate exam, adding that she could not wait to show what she has been working on.

Savannah further said she is more excited to help people find their dream homes. Her post generated loads of reactions from her numerous fans, who took turns to congratulate her.

One of Savannah's fans said they knew she had it in her and are proud of her, while another fan commented on her looks, saying she looked like her mom in the picture.

Kerdiles was also among the comments and told Savannah, "So proud of you babe!" before adding that all the time and hard work she put into her preparations were worth it.

Savannah admitted that the past three years had been some of the best of her life.

Kerdile's endearing comment and use of "Babe" when referring to Savannah caused some fans to think the former couple is back together. However, Savannah's representative confirmed that they are not, but remain good friends.

In September, Savannah announced via a post on her Instagram page that she and Kerdiles were ending their relationship almost two years after they got engaged.

Nick Kerdiles' comment on Savannah Chrisley's Instagram post | Photo: Instagram / savannahchrisley

The reality star said there is no hatred between them, which she noted made their split much more difficult. She further said that they have nothing but admiration, respect, and love for each other but that it was time for them to move forward individually.

Savannah admitted that the past three years had been some of the best of her life, adding that she is trusting God to turn the hardship into something beautiful. She concluded by asking fans to be kind with her announcement.
