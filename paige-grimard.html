Biography

Paige Grimard was born in Ohio on May 16, 1989. Dancer and model with the Exit12 Dance Company, she is best known for her appearances in the 2011 Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show and for being cast in the off-broadway stage show Awesome 80’s Prom. She starred opposite Monroe Robertson as Gigi Hadid in the parody of Zayn Malik‘s debut solo single “Pillowtalk.” A trained ballerina, she studied at BalletMet in Columbus before attending the University of Cincinnati. Two years later, she was back in ballet, studying with the Richmond Ballet.
