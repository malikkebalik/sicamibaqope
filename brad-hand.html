Who is Brad Hand?

Brad Hand is an American professional baseball pitcher best known for his work with the Major League Baseball (MLB) Philadelphia Phillies. He previously played in Major League Baseball for the Miami Marlins, San Diego Padres, Cleveland Indians, Washington Nationals, Toronto Blue Jays, and New York Mets.

What is the Net Worth of Brad Hand? Salary, Earnings

Brad Hand is a professional baseball pitcher with a 20 million dollar net worth as of 2023. He is said to supplement his income through endorsement deals. Among the brands he frequently promotes on Instagram are Golf Pride, Costa Sunglasses, Stance, and others. He is currently living a high life.

Where was Brad Hand born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Brad Hand was born on March 20, 1990, in Minneapolis, Minnesota. He is of American descent and of American-White ancestry. He will be 33 years old in 2023. Lon and Barb Hand, his parents, gave birth to him. His family moved around Minnesota during his childhood, stopping in West St. Paul and Eagan before settling in Chaska just before he started high school.

Brad was a three-sport varsity athlete at Chaska High School, earning honors in American football, ice hockey, and baseball. He finished his senior year with an 8-2 record, two saves, and a 0.61 ERA in 68 innings pitched, allowing only six earned runs. He has committed to play college baseball for the Arizona State Sun Devils.
