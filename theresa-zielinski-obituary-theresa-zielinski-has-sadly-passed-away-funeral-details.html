Theresa Zielinski Obituary, Theresa Zielinski Has Sadly Passed Away, Funeral Details

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, September 12, 2024
0SHARESShareTweet

Theresa Zielinski Obituary, Death – Theresa E. Zielinski, a beloved resident of Niagara Falls, NY, passed away on November 15, 2023, at the age of 93. Born on December 19, 1929, to Casimir and Karolina Walasek, Theresa was a proud graduate of Niagara Falls HS. She initially worked at the Carborundum Company before dedicating her time to raising a family. Theresa was a woman of diverse interests and passions. She found joy in the simple pleasures of life, such as reading novels, preparing delicious Polish meals, and dancing to Polka music. A devoted fan of the Buffalo Bills, she enthusiastically cheered for her favorite team.

However, her greatest source of happiness came from the moments spent with her children, Donna, Paula, and Jim (Sandra) Zielinski, along with her cherished grandchildren, Sara, Leah, and James. Theresa was one of 11 siblings, and she treasured the bond she shared with each of them. Predeceased by her husband, Emil Zielinski, and siblings William and Henry Walasek, Sophie Bozek, Katherine Apen, Clara Zankowski, Celia Wisz, Estelle Hriczak, Martha Bulinski, Renee Dunaj, and Bernadette Lachowski, Theresa leaves behind a legacy of love and family.

The family expresses gratitude to the caregivers at Schoellkopf Health Center, where Theresa spent her last months surrounded by the love of family, friends, patients, and her grandpup “Duck.” Friends are invited to pay their respects on Monday, November 20th, from 4-8 PM at ZAJAC FUNERAL HOME INC., located at 319 24th Street, Niagara Falls, NY. A Mass of Christian burial will take place at 10:30 AM on Tuesday, November 21st, at Divine Mercy Parish, 2437 Niagara Street, Niagara Falls, NY, officiated by Rev. Duane Klizek. Theresa will be laid to rest in Holy Trinity Cemetery, Lewiston, NY, following the Mass. The community is invited to join in remembering and celebrating the life of Theresa E. Zielinski.
