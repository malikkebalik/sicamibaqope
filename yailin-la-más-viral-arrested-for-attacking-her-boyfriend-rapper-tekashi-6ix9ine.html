Yailin La Ms Viral arrested for attacking her boyfriend, rapper Tekashi 6ix9ine

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Domestic violence is common these days and the victim could be either party involved. American female rapper, Yailin La Más Viral was arrested recently for having physically assaulted her boyfriend, rapper Tekashi 6ix9ine. She had also destroyed his costly Bentley in anger.

Yailin La Más Viral and her arrest

Yailin La Más Viral is an American female rapper whose real name is Jeorjina Guillermo-Diaz. She has been dating American rapper Tekashi 6ix9ine for some time now. And now, the Palm Beach County Sheriff’s Office arrested Yailin from the couple’s Palm Beach home. The charges were of aggravated battery using a deadly weapon, battery, and obstructing justice.

The police also framed charges of property damage-criminal mischief against her since Yailin had deliberately and in anger caused significant damage to the expensive Bentley car of Tekashi.

The arrest report did not mention Tekashi’s name as the alleged victim but has put his profession as that of a singer. But the videos circulated on social media of the attack incident show clearly that the victim is Tekashi. Tekashi’s real name is Daniel Hernandez.

What the videos on social media reveal?

In the video, it can be seen that Tekashi has donned a red and orange sweatshirt with black pants and white Crocs. And Yailin is seen attacking him multiple times. She has blue hair during the incident.

The police report states that deputies arrived on the scene of the crime after receiving a call for domestic violence. The alleged victim told them that he and Yailin had a verbal argument that lasted the whole day. The reason for the argument was that Yailin believed that Tekashi is looking at other women.

Tekashi tried to tell her that what she was thinking was not right and that they should cool down. But that only angered Yailin further and she began hitting him hard and multiple times in the house’s driveway.

Yailin caught him by his hair. Later, she hit the driver side mirror of his expensive care and broke it. She also kicked several times the multi-colored car of Tekashi. In order to protect himself, Teksahi ran into his garage. But Yailin prevented him from closing the garage road. She then took a 2×4 and hit Tekashi with it.

More details on the attack videos

In desperation, Tekashi dialed 911 for help. But Yailin hit his hand holding the phone with the 2×4 and the phone fell to the ground. Yailin again turned her attention to his car and broke its gear shifter. She picked up a ground stake and broke the windshield with it. In order to make her stop the violence. Tekashi bear hugged her but she started pulling his hair instead.

Yailin’s say was that Tekashi wanted to go to a recording studio and she did not want to go there. She wanted to leave but could not do so because he had her money, passport and phone. The report adds:

“Yailin decided to kick the driver’s side mirror and hit the windshield to cause him financial hardship because he would not give her money or her passport,”

Yailin is out on USD 9000 bond. Her lawyer, Lorenzo Perez said:

“This case will be resolved in a positive way, I’m sure, especially when the victim in this case, Mr. Hernandez, is asking the prosecution to drop the case, and he’s also here waiting to make sure everything’s going smoothly for my client to be released,”

Yailin had a previous marriage to Puerto Rican rapper Anuel AA. They split in February 2023. She and Tekashi collaborated on a song in May 2023 and are since then dating each other.
