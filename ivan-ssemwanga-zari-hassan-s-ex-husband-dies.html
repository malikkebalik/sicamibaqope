Ivan Ssemwanga, Zari Hassan's Ex-husband Dies

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Diamond Platnumz’s baby mama, Zari Hassan lost her ex husband, Ivan Ssemwanga, who was recently hospitalized with a heart condition.

The father of three was suffering from coronary artery disease and was admitted at Steve Biko hospital, Pretoria. He later went into a coma.

Ivan was a Ugandan businessman but was based in South Africa. He took his last breathe today and Zari broke the news on Instagram saying:

“God loves those that are special and that’s exactly who you were & I guess that’s why he wanted you to himself. You have touched and helped thousands, you did wonders and I remember you telling me “life is too short let me live it to the fullest”, this very dark hour it makes sense why you always said those words to me.

“To your sons, you were a hero-some kind of superman. Anyone who has ever been in your presence knows what a charming person you were. You will be missed and remembered in so many ways. You were IVAN THE GREAT! Rest in peace DON.”
