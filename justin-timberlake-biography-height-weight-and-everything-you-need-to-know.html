Justin Timberlake Biography, Height, Weight, And Everything You Need To Know

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Justin Timberlake is an American singer, songwriter, record producer, and actor. Before launching a lucrative solo career, he first gained notoriety as a member of the boy band NSYNC. Here is a quick biography of Justin Timberlake that includes his stature, weight, and other pertinent information:

Justin Timberlake Career

Boy band NSYNC: Timberlake rose to stardom in the late 1990s as one of the group’s lead vocalists. With classics like “Bye Bye Bye” and “Tearin’ Up My Heart,” the trio enjoyed great popularity.

Solo Career: Timberlake started a solo career in 2002 and published his debut album, “Justified,” which was well received by critics and was a commercial success. His solo singles include “Cry Me a River,” “Rock Your Body,” and “SexyBack.”

Acting Career: Timberlake has dabbled in acting and has made appearances in a number of movies, such as “The Social Network,” “Friends with Benefits,” and “Trolls,” where he gave the voice of the character Branch.

Justin Timberlake Weight And Height

Justin Timberlake is roughly 6 feet 1 inch (185 cm) tall. Although Justin Timberlake’s exact weight is not known to the general public, he is renowned for maintaining an athletic and fit figure.

Justin Timberlake Personal Life

Relationships: Timberlake and actress Jessica Biel have two children together after their 2012 wedding.

Philanthropy: Throughout his career, he has been involved in a number of charitable projects, such as his work with the Justin Timberlake Foundation, which promotes music education for young people.

Justin Timberlake Awards and Successes

Timberlake has won various honors during his career, including 10 Grammy Awards, four Emmy Awards, and multiple Billboard Music Awards.

In addition, he has been honored for his acting abilities and won the Primetime Emmy Award for his guest spot on “Saturday Night Live.”

Despite having a career spanning several decades, Justin Timberlake is still a well-known personality in the entertainment sphere.
