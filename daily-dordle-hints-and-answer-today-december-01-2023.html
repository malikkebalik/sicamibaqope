Daily Dordle Gameplay

Dordle is a game that's a lot like Wordle. In Dordle, you can play in two different ways: Free Dordle or Daily Dordle. The difference between them is that in the Daily mode, everyone has to guess the same word for the entire day. In the Free mode, you get random puzzles to solve whenever you want.

The rules are the same in both modes. It's like Wordle, but with a twist. You have to guess two words at the same time. For each of these words, you have seven possible options. The game helps you by using different colors to show if a letter is in the word and whether it's in the right place. Yellow means the letter is in the word but in the wrong place, and green means it's in the correct spot.

It's a fun game 26at challenges your word skills and thinking. You need to figure out both words using the clues the game gives you.
