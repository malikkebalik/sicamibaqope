Glenn Plummer Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Who is Glenn Plummer?

Glenn E. Plummer is an American film and TV actor. Glenn is well-known for playing the role of Dennis Jenkins in 9-1-1.

He won Black Reel Awards in Network/Cable- Best Supporting Actor for The Corner.

He is working as Thomas in the pre-production of Fuhgedd About It.

Glenn Plummer- Early Life and Professional Life

Glenn Plummer was born on 18th August 1961 in Richmond, California, US.

He has been active in a TV career for four decades. In 1987, he played the role of Willy Johnson in Hands of a Stanger. 

After that, he appeared in Who’s That Girl, Bad Attitude, Beauty and the Beast, Hearts of Stone, Hostile Witness, Tour of Duty, Colors, China Beach, Funny Farm, and others.

In the 1990s, Glenn Plummer has appeared in many roles in Heat Wave, Cop Rock, Pastime, Murderous Vision, Wenlock, Trespass, Menace II Society, Dream On, Snake Eyes, There’s No Place Like Homegirl, Speed, Strange Days, Showgirls, The Substitute, Small Time and others.

After that, he made an appearance in Sugar Valentine, Constellation, Go for Broke 2, Brothers in Arms, Saw II, Bones, It Ain’t Easy, Vegas Vampires, ER, Dexter, The Longshots, Raising the Bar, Sons of Anarchy, Cold Case and others.

Recent TV appearance, Post-production, Pre-production

Recently, Glenn Plummer has appeared in Measure of Faith, Monsters in the Woods, Scandalous, Imperial Dreams, The Other Side, Teeth and Blood, Battle Creek, Welcome to the Family, The Sweet Life, Family Time, Gifted, Greenleaf, Shameless, Crimson Blues, Fuhgedd About It, 4 Kings, One and the Same and others.

Director

His director credit includes The Sanctuary, Calista House, 7 Deadly Sins, F8TH, and VooDoo Curse: The Giddeh.

Producer

Glenn Plummer has produced Calista House, Teeth and Blood, E! Cortez, VooDoo Curse: The Giddeh, and Love Beat the Hell Outta Me.

Writer

Glenn Plummer has worked as a writer in Calista House, Teeth and Blood, and VooDoo Curse: The Giddeh.

Awards, Nomination

  • 2001: Black Reel Awards for Network/Cable – Best Supporting Actor
  • 1993: Chicago Film Critics Association Awards for Most Promising Actor
  • 1992: Film Independent Spirit Awards for Best Supporting Male

How much is the net worth of Glenn Plummer?

His estimated net worth of Glenn is $800 thousand US. As an American actor, he earns an average salary of $19,647 – $210,569 US.

Glenn Plummer played the role of Greg Cullen in Gifted which collected $43 million US. Similarly, he played Simon in The Other Side which made a box office collection of $14.3 million US.

Body Measurement

Glenn has black hair and dark eyes. He is 5 feet 8 inches tall.
